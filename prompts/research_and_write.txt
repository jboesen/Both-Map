### Depth contract — this is the most important constraint
The reader already knows the domain. They do not need:
- A definition of the topic or its components
- A history of how we got here
- An explanation of why this matters or why they should care

What they want instead:

- If two things are in tension, don't explain the tension — analyze what resolves it and what that resolution reveals
